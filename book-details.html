The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott FitzGerald

Publisher: Charles Scribner's Sons

Year: 1925

ISBN: 9781982146702

Genre: Classic, Literary Fiction, Jazz Age novel

Status: Considered one of the greatest American novels of the 20th century widely studied and admired

Description

The Great Gatsby is novel set in the 1920s in the New York City and Long Island, exploring themes of love, greed, class, and the American Dream. The story revolves around Jay Gatsby's mysterious wealth and his obsession with winning back his lost love , Daisy Buchanan. The novel is known for its vivid portrayal of the Jazz Age and its commentary on social class, identity, and morality.
